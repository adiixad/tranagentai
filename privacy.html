Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026. This policy explains what personal data TrainAgentAI collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as account details, contact information, and contributor identity verification data, as well as information generated through your use of the platform, such as task activity, submissions, and device or log data.

2. How we use information

We use personal data to operate and improve the platform, match contributors to tasks, process payments, verify identity, communicate with you, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of information

We share data with enterprise and AI lab customers only as needed to deliver contracted services, with payment processors to complete contributor payouts, and with vendors who support our infrastructure under confidentiality obligations. We do not sell personal data.

4. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide our services, meet legal and contractual obligations, and resolve disputes, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

5. Security

We apply encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, and regular audits to protect personal data. Further detail is available on our Security & Compliance page.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. You can exercise these rights by contacting privacy@trainagentai.com.

7. International transfers

As a global platform, personal data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses to protect transferred data.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or by email before they take effect.

10. Contact us

For questions about this policy or to exercise your privacy rights, contact privacy@trainagentai.com.
